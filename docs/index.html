Our Mission

The Ecology Project is an ambitious initiative designed to bridge the gap between urban development and environmental preservation. By utilizing state-of-the-art Random Forest and XGBoost models, we analyze critical factors like Greenness, Urban Density, and Air Quality (PM2.5) to provide actionable insights for city planners and environmentalists.
